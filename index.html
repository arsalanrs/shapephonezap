Simple steps (for Sam)
  1. Type the Lead ID. Click Load from Shape and wait until the lead table appears.
  2. Add your call transcripts: paste into the box and/or drop files in the upload area. Then click Run AI extraction and wait for the review table.
  3. Read each row. You can click Accept all suggestions or type what you want in the Approved column. When it looks right, click Submit updates to Shape — that sends your approved changes through the Quest Rock (QR) engine into Shape so the lead is updated.

If you see a red error message under a step, read it out loud or screenshot it for help.
